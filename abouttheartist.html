My name is Chloe Gunning, I am a 22 year old artist that creates both digital art and acrylic art. My main art style is abstract, but I am also working on diversifying my art style to a realism style.

As of right now, I only plan to showcase my art, I am not ready to sell anything. I want to broaden my art style and genre and just overall grow as an artist.  I also want to create more art pieces and have a collection of 25 – 30 pieces before I think about selling them. I will definitely sell my art in the future, but for now I just want to create a space where my art can be seen. Here are what I currently have on my portfolio.

  • 10 handpainted, abstract projects
